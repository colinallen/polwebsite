General Help

The Power of Logic web site is primarily designed for students to assess their understanding of materials in the text book and to practice the new skills taught in courses based on the book.

The purpose of online help is not to provide instruction in logic, but to explain how to use the components of the program.
Menu items are accessed by clicking either on the small graphic accompanying each item or on the text itself.

Typing special symbols

Some of the symbols used in chapters 7-9 are not on the keyboard and cannot easily be shown in html. Here is what you should type instead:
→ arrow "->" (dash + greater than), or simply ">"
↔ double arrow "<->" (less than + dash + greater than), or simply "<"
∙ conjunction (dot) "." (a period)
∨ disjunction (wedge) "v" (lower case letter vee)
∃ existential quantifier "$" — think $ome!
∴ therefore ":." or ".:" (colon and period, in either order)
